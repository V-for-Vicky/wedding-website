How to Reach Litochoro?

By Car: Rent a car from SKG Airport and drive approximately 1.5 hours along the E75 highway.

By Train: Take a train from Thessaloniki Railway Station to Litochoro Station, then a short taxi ride into town.

By Bus: Use the KTEL bus service from Thessaloniki Bus Station to Litochoro. The trip takes about 1.5 hours.

By Taxi: Hire a taxi directly from SKG Airport to Litochoro for convenience.

*We strongly advise you to have a car the day of the wedding, as the locations of interest are not whithin walking distance.
